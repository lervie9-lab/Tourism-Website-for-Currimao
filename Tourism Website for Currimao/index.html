Welcome to Currimao

The Coral City of the North awaits you with beautiful beaches, rich culture, and delicious local cuisine!

Attractions

Currilights

Experience the enchanting glow of Currilights as the sun sets over Currimao’s stunning coastline. Perfect for evening strolls, photography, and moments of tranquility amidst nature’s beauty.

Pangil Rock Formation

Marvel at the majestic Pangil Rock Formation, a natural landmark perfect for sightseeing, climbing, and capturing stunning coastal views.

Tabacalera Ruins

Step back in time at the historic Tabacalera Ruins, where remnants of old tobacco warehouses tell the story of Currimao’s rich heritage and colonial past.

Torre Ruins

Discover the Torre Ruins, a historic site offering a glimpse into Currimao’s past, with its weathered structures standing as silent witnesses to history.

Currimao Bypass

Enjoy a scenic drive along the Currimao Bypass, where coastal views meet serene landscapes. Perfect for road trips, biking, or a relaxing escape from the city.

Currimao Port

Experience the vibrant Currimao Port, where daily fishing activities meet majestic cruise ships docking along the coast. Enjoy fresh seafood, stunning seaside views, and a lively maritime atmosphere.

Currimao Sunset

Witness breathtaking sunsets over Currimao’s coastline, where golden skies meet tranquil waters. Perfect for evening strolls, photography, and peaceful moments by the sea.

Paguludan River

Relax by the serene Paguludan River, surrounded by lush greenery and calm waters. Ideal for nature walks, picnics, and unwinding in a peaceful riverside setting.

Accommodation

D'Coral Beach Resort

Unwind at D'Coral Beach Resort, where soft sands meet crystal-clear waters. Perfect for swimming, beach activities, and relaxing stays with family and friends.

Playa Tropical

Escape to Playa Tropical, a pristine beach paradise in Currimao. Enjoy golden sands, gentle waves, and a perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and beachside relaxation.

Sitio Remedios

Discover the charm of Sitio Remedios, a quiet coastal spot in Currimao perfect for relaxing, exploring hidden beaches, and enjoying peaceful seaside views.

Camp de Beleza

Experience a relaxing getaway at Camp de Beleza, where nature, comfort, and outdoor activities come together. Perfect for camping, bonding with friends, and enjoying Currimao’s scenic beauty.

Pangil Beach Resort

Relax at Pangil Beach, a serene coastal escape in Currimao. Enjoy soft sands, gentle waves, and a perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and unwinding by the sea.

Street Food & Local Delicacies

Irene's Tupig

Taste the sweet tradition of Irene’s Tupig, a local delicacy made from glutinous rice and coconut. Perfect for souvenirs, snacks, and experiencing Currimao’s rich culinary heritage.

Seawall Boulevard at Night

Take an evening stroll along Seawall Boulevard at night, where the sea breeze, city lights, and vibrant street food scene create a lively and unforgettable experience.

Festivals & Important Days

Dinaklisan Festival

Join the fun at Dinaklisan Festival's colorful parades, lively music, and tasty local treats await in Currimao!

Foundation Day

Celebrate Currimao’s founding every January 21 with colorful parades, lively cultural events, and heartwarming community gatherings that showcase the town’s rich heritage.

Festivals & Important Days

Fishing Industry

Explore Currimao’s thriving fishing industry, where local fishermen bring in fresh catches daily. Witness traditional fishing methods, bustling fish markets, and the vital role this industry plays in the community.
